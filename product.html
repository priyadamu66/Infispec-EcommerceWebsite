Product Page

Creating an effective product page for a new user on an e-commerce website is crucial for providing a positive shopping experience and encouraging conversions.
The main purpose of the product page is to
  • Display high-quality images of the product from different angles.
  • Provide a concise and compelling product title.
  • Include a clear and detailed product description, highlighting key features and benefits.
  • Specify important details such as size, color, material, and any other relevant information

  • In this product page we can also apply the filter for searching a specific product
  • Upon using the filter the particular product category will be displayed
  • On selecting the particular product the details of the selected product will be displayed
  • After selecting user will be navigated to the Cart and Checkout process
  • After selecting the product user can also submit the review.
